We are Covid safe...

We aim to do everything we can to keep you and our cleaners safe from coronavirus. Your cleaner can clean your home at a time convenient to you. If you are able to go out then your home can be cleaned in your absence. Alternatively, if you are home during your clean, you can stay in one room and your cleaner will maintain the recommended 2 metre social distance.
